What We Offer

Our Services

Free resources for buyers. Structured support for housing organizations. All designed around the needs of affordable homeownership.

Two Audiences. One Platform.

Services for Buyers & Organizations

We serve the full affordable housing ecosystem — connecting buyers who need homes with organizations that have them.

For Homebuyers

Buyer Services

Everything you need to explore, qualify, and move toward homeownership — completely free, with no obligation at any stage.

Property Access

Browse available affordable homes, including properties not listed on the open MLS market. Each listing includes photos, pricing, and program guidelines.

  • Affordable homes including off-MLS inventory
  • Photos and virtual tours where available
  • Clear pricing and affordability guidelines for each property

Free Interest List

Understand where you stand before committing to a formal application. Our Interest List process is designed to give you honest, realistic information — nothing more.

  • Estimated monthly payment calculations
  • Down payment projections based on property and program
  • Debt-to-income ratio estimates
  • Basic eligibility review against program guidelines
  • No credit check. No documentation. No obligation.

Education & Process Guidance

Many first-time buyers don't know what they don't know. We provide clear, plain-English guidance on every step of the process.

  • Step-by-step overview of the purchase process
  • Explanation of income limits and compliance requirements
  • Realistic timeline expectations
  • Referral guidance to qualified lenders familiar with affordable housing programs

For Organizations

Partner Services

Structured marketing, buyer screening, and sales support designed specifically for affordable housing providers.

Marketing & Exposure

Get your affordable housing inventory in front of the right audience — income-qualified, motivated buyers who are actively searching.

  • Dedicated property listing pages with photos and details
  • Targeted buyer audience at 50–120% AMI
  • Professional digital promotion of your inventory
  • Option to list on the MLS or market exclusively through this platform first

Buyer Pre-Qualification Screening

We handle the initial review so you spend time only with buyers who meet your program's basic requirements.

  • Initial income and eligibility review
  • Structured intake forms tailored to program requirements
  • Organized buyer pipeline — not raw, unfiltered leads
  • Compliance-minded documentation flow

Consulting & Sales Support

From strategy to buyer communication, we provide hands-on support to help your affordable housing program succeed.

  • Affordable housing positioning strategy
  • Process development and documentation flow design
  • Buyer education coordination
  • Sales tracking and ongoing buyer communication support
