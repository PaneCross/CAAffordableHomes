Current Opportunities

Affordable Homeownership Opportunities

The homes featured on this page are part of affordable housing opportunities with income and eligibility guidelines.

Some properties are listed on the MLS, while others are offered through housing programs and may not be publicly advertised. Availability, pricing, and eligibility can vary based on program requirements and may change over time.

Joining the Interest List does not guarantee eligibility or approval. It simply begins the process of being considered and matched with opportunities you may qualify for.

Joining the Interest List allows us to match you with potential opportunities based on general program criteria. Final eligibility and selection are determined by the housing program administrator in accordance with program guidelines.
