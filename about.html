About CA Affordable Homes

We are committed to helping income-qualified homebuyers in the communities we serve access a clear, honest path to homeownership.

Our Story

When We Started

Over 15 years ago, I stepped into the nonprofit affordable housing space and quickly witnessed something unforgettable: the moment a family realizes homeownership is finally within reach.

A set of keys represents so much more than a home. It represents stability, peace of mind, and the beginning of a new chapter.

Since then, we've had the privilege of walking alongside first-time and low-income homebuyers as they navigate what can often feel like an overwhelming process. Again and again, we've seen the same truth unfold: with the right guidance and support, doors begin to open that once felt out of reach.

At the heart of everything we do is a simple belief, when a family has a stable home, it can transform their future. It creates security, opportunity, and the kind of foundation that strengthens not just households, but entire communities.

Mission

Why We Exist

Many of the affordable homeownership opportunities we work with are never publicly listed. For most families, they would never even know they exist. That's where we step in. We help bridge the gap between these opportunities and the people who may qualify for them, working alongside the organizations that make these homes possible.

Our mission is simple: to guide individuals and families through this process with honesty, support, and commitment, and help turn what once felt out of reach into a place they can truly call home.

What Makes Us Different

  • Access to properties not publicly advertised, including city and developer-direct opportunities
  • Join our interest list with no cost, no credit pull, no obligation
  • Structured buyer intake forms built for compliance and housing programs
Who We Serve

Buyers & Organizations

We serve two distinct but equally important groups, the families looking for a home, and the organizations that provide them.

For Buyers

First-Time Homebuyers

  • Households with moderate incomes (typically 50-120% of Area Median Income)
  • Want to understand eligibility before a formal application
  • Need clarity on costs, timelines, and what to expect
  • Looking for affordable homes designed for owner-occupants
  • Coordinating pre-screened buyers with lenders in accordance with specific program guidelines
For Organizations

Builders, Developers & Municipalities

  • Affordable housing developers with inventory to fill
  • New construction builders with income-restricted units
  • City and county housing programs
  • Nonprofit housing organizations
Our Services

What We Offer

We serve the full affordable housing ecosystem, connecting buyers who need homes with organizations that have them.

For Buyers

Buyer Services

  • Education and guidance
  • Step-by-step overview of the purchase process
  • Explanation of income limits and compliance requirements
  • Realistic timeline expectations
  • Referral guidance to qualified and approved lenders familiar with affordable housing programs
For Organizations

Partner Services

  • Private outreach to pre-screened interest list members before broader market exposure, or full MLS listing with our support
  • Buyer Pre-Qualification Screening
  • Structured intake forms tailored to program requirements
  • Buyer education coordination
  • Sales tracking and ongoing buyer communication support
We are not a lender and we are not a credit repair service. We are a resource and connection point, helping buyers understand their position and helping housing providers reach eligible households. Joining the interest list is informational only. Final eligibility and approval is determined by the appropriate lender and/or housing authority.
