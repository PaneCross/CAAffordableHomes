Our Story

About CA Affordable Homes

We believe every income-qualified Californian deserves a clear, honest path to homeownership.

Mission

Why We Exist

We believe affordable homeownership should be transparent, understandable, and achievable. Our mission is to create a centralized, easy-to-navigate platform where income-qualified buyers can explore affordable housing opportunities without confusion, pressure, or hidden requirements.

Many affordable homes never reach traditional real estate platforms. We help bridge that gap — connecting qualified buyers with available properties while supporting the organizations that create them.

What Makes Us Different

  • Focused exclusively on income-qualified, affordable housing opportunities
  • Access to off-MLS and developer-direct listings not found elsewhere
  • Pre-screening with no credit pull, no documents, no cost
  • Structured buyer intake built for compliance and housing programs
Who We Serve

Buyers & Organizations

We serve two distinct but equally important groups — the families looking for a home, and the organizations that create them.

For Buyers

First-Time Homebuyers

  • Haven't owned a home in the past three years
  • Household income between 50–120% of the Area Median Income
  • Want to understand eligibility before a formal application
  • Need clarity on costs, timelines, and what to expect
  • May not know which assistance programs they qualify for
  • Looking for homes priced for real people, not investors
For Organizations

Builders, Developers & Municipalities

  • Affordable housing developers with inventory to fill
  • New construction builders with income-restricted units
  • City and county housing programs
  • Nonprofit housing organizations
  • Community development corporations (CDCs)
  • Government-sponsored affordable housing programs
How We Work

Our Approach

Transparency

Clear guidelines, realistic cost estimates, and no surprises. What you see is what you get.

Accessibility

Free pre-screening with no credit pull, no documentation required, and no obligation at any stage.

Efficiency

Streamlined buyer qualification before moving to a formal application — saving time for everyone.

Professional Representation

Structured marketing and compliance-minded intake processes built around affordable housing requirements.

We are not a lender and we are not a credit repair service. We are a resource and connection point — helping buyers understand their position and helping housing providers reach eligible households. All pre-screening is informational only. Final eligibility and approval is determined by the appropriate lender and/or housing authority.
